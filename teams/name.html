History of Our Name

The name Power Scare came from aiweirdness.com which used a recurrent neural network to create names using a list of 6,197 names of other teams. We found the name Power Scare, and liked it quite a bit. However in 2019-2020 we merged the two FLL teams together, and we had to chose a new name after the merger. Some suggestions were Cyber Knights-Power Scare, Power Nights and Cyber Scare, and we all know how that turned out.
