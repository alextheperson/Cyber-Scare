Varchion Hunter

Our team created this app for their innovation project to help people be more active. It's a Pokemon Go-style location based game where you find artifacts from the lost Yokin civilization. When you get close enough, you'll be able to find and collect the artifact in Augmented Reality mode.

The Yokins were an ancient civilization. It turns out that this ancient civilization left remnants of their culture all around us - if you look closely you can find vases, ancient swords, and perhaps even a treasured Varchion Teleportation Staff.



The development team is looking for people to test the app and give us some feedback. We're looking for feature suggestions, bug reports, and any other ideas you might have.

You can install the app with Testflight for iOS.

On Android, install the latest APK build.
